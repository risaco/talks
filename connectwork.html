How do we
initiate and cultivate
these connections?

Have a plan when you can

Set a goal to meet 10 new people

Don't let titles dissuade you

You don't know who or what they know yet

Fight fear, make the first move

Remember to Pac-Man!

Small talk leads to more talk

Start simple, the conversation will grow organically

In a room full of them...

be a YOU

Your real self will serve you far better than your representative self

Be a genuine listener

Be present in the conversation so you can remember the connection

Consider how you can help

One-sided relationships don't last long

Trade contact info

You can't help or be helped if you can't get in touch

Follow up and follow through!
